Introduction

SJTU-Spark is a distributed computing platform sponsored by the Center of Intelligent Internet of Things. Our team aims to realize massive-data related algorithms on Apache Spark, a state-of-the-art computing framework. Most of our effors are devoted to the high performance backend of the academic map system --- SJTU-Acemap.
